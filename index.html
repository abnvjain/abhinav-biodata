Abhinav was born on 29th November 2002, in the early hours before dawn, in Gulabpura, Bijaynagar — a town that carries the unhurried warmth of Rajasthan in every season. He grew up in Bhilwara, raised with values his family holds above all else: sincerity, discipline, and deep affection for the people around him.

He completed his B.Tech. and moved to Bengaluru, where he now works as a Business Analyst at Bewakoof Brands Pvt. Ltd. Colleagues know him for a sharp, methodical mind that finds clarity where others find confusion. Those closer to him know another side too — the one who comes home to write songs, because numbers and melodies have always coexisted in him.

Abhinav is, at his core, a person of few words and considered action. He carries Rajasthan in his roots and navigates the world with quiet steadiness. He is his parents' only child — and in that, one finds both the love given to him and the responsibility he has taken on his own shoulders.
