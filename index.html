Parichay

Abhinav was born on the 29th of November, 2002, in the early hours before dawn, in Gulabpura, Bijaynagar — a town that carries the unhurried warmth of Rajasthan in every season. He grew up in Bhilwara, raised with values that his family has always placed above all else: sincerity, discipline, and a deep affection for the people around him.

He completed his B.Tech. and, with quiet ambition, made his way to Bengaluru, where he works today as a Business Analyst at Bewakoof Brands Pvt. Ltd. Those who work alongside him know him for his sharp, methodical mind — the kind that finds clarity where others find confusion. Those who know him personally know something else too: that the same person who spends his day in data and strategy comes home to write songs, because numbers and melodies have always coexisted in him without contradiction.

Abhinav is, at his core, a person of few words and considered action. He carries Rajasthan in his roots and navigates the world with the steadiness of someone who knows where he comes from. He is his parents' only child, and in that, one finds both the love lavished upon him and the responsibility he has quietly taken on his own shoulders.
